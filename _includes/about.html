About



In December of 2004, four friends decided to change the way they were living their lives forever by forming a band and creating rock music without limitations regardless of genre or style. They picked up their instruments, got to work and went on a whirlwind campaign that lasted years to fulfill what they came to do.

Their original EP includes the powerhouse "Barrys Song," a track specifically dedicated to a slain friend and "Confession," open thoughts about an ex-loved one. My Private Hell was based out of Los Angeles and heavily expressed strong convictions about everyday struggles of life and all of the different elements it encompassed.
